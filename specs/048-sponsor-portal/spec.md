# Feature Specification: Sponsor Portal

## Summary

Deliver a sponsor-facing portal area with sponsorship overview, quota context, and coupon visibility.

## Requirements

- The system must provide a sponsor-facing entry point backed by real sponsor and coupon data.
- Sponsors must be able to view their sponsorship status, selected quota, and generated coupons.
- The MVP authentication approach must be pragmatic and aligned with the current backend capabilities.
- The implementation must include automated tests and technical status sync.
